We will use the auto dataset. It includes variable price:
~~~~
<<dd_do>>
webuse auto, clear
summarize price
<</dd_do>>
~~~~


The mean of price is <<dd_display: %9.0g r(mean)>>. We will also check the relationship of **mpg** and **weight**.


<<dd_do: quietly>>
scatter mpg weight, mcolor(blue%50)
<</dd_do>>

<<dd_graph : saving("graph.svg") alt("scatter mpg price") replace height(400)>>

and via a linear regression:
<<dd_do:quietly>>
regress mpg weight
<</dd_do>>

<<dd_do: nocommand>>

_coef_table, markdown
<</dd_do>>
